## ACCOMMODATION DETAILS & BOOKING LINKS

### Tokyo: Dormy Inn Asakusa (4 nights)
- **Why**: Private room, rooftop onsen bath, free late-night ramen, near Senso-ji
- **Price**: ~$65–85/night for double room
- **Book on**: Agoda (often cheapest) > Booking.com > hotespa.net direct
- **Links**:
  - https://www.agoda.com (search "Dormy Inn Asakusa")
  - https://www.booking.com (search "Dormy Inn Asakusa")
  - https://www.hotespa.net/hotels/ (direct, check member rates)

### Takayama Night 1: Traditional Ryokan (1 night, dinner+breakfast)
- **Why**: THE quintessential Japan experience. Tatami room, futon, kaiseki dinner, onsen
- **Top picks** (search on Japanican.com for Apr 9, filter "dinner & breakfast"):
  - **Oyado Koto no Yume** — renovated merchant house, Hida beef kaiseki, ~$130-180/room
  - **Sumiyoshi Ryokan** — family-run, warm, near morning market, ~$110-150/room
  - **Tanabe Ryokan** — budget ryokan, central, authentic, ~$100-130/room
  - **Honjin Hiranoya Annex** — splurge option, excellent Hida beef, ~$160-220/room
- **Book on**: https://www.japanican.com/en/ (best ryokan platform)
- **Remember**: Price INCLUDES dinner and breakfast for both of you = saves ~$40-60 on food that day

### Takayama Night 2: K's House Takayama (1 night)
- **Why**: Budget-friendly, traditional building, great common area, bike rental
- **Price**: ~$45-65 for private Japanese room (tatami/futon)
- **Book on**: https://kshouse.jp/takayama-e/ (direct is best)

### Kanazawa: HATCHi Kanazawa (1 night)
- **Why**: Design hotel, near Omicho Market, stylish common area
- **Price**: ~$55-80 for private double
- **Book on**: https://thesharehotels.com/hatchi/en/ (direct)
- **Backup**: Kaname Inn Tatemachi (craft beer bar downstairs, ~$40-65)

### Kyoto: Piece Hostel Sanjo (3 nights) + Airbnb Machiya (2 nights)
- **Piece Hostel**: Top-rated in Japan (9.0+ rating), private double rooms, free morning coffee, near Gion/Keihan Line
  - **Price**: ~$65-85/night
  - **Book on**: https://www.piecekyoto.com/en/ (direct, also Hostelworld/Booking.com)
  - **WARNING**: Private rooms sell out fast in April. Book TODAY.
- **Airbnb Machiya**: Traditional 100+ year old Kyoto townhouse, sliding screens, garden, full kitchen
  - **Price**: ~$70-100/night
  - **Search on**: Airbnb → "Kyoto" → filter: Entire place, search "machiya" in keyword
  - **Best areas**: Higashiyama (near temples), Nakagyo (central), Nishijin (quieter, cheaper)
  - **Look for**: Superhost, 4.7+ stars, 10+ reviews, registration number starting with "M"

**Kyoto backup if Piece Hostel is full**:
- K's House Kyoto — $45-60/night, private Japanese room, rooftop terrace
  - https://kshouse.jp/kyoto-e/
- Nui-like option: Len Kyoto Kawaramachi — design hostel, similar vibe, ~$55-75

### Tokyo Final: Toyoko Inn Shinagawa (1-2 nights)
- **Why**: Clean, cheap, free breakfast, walking distance to Keikyu Line → Haneda (15 min)
- **Price**: ~$45-70/night
- **Book on**: https://www.toyoko-inn.com/eng/ (direct, get free club card for discount)
- **Specific property**: Toyoko Inn Shinagawa-eki Takanawaguchi (right at the station)
- **Upgrade option**: Dormy Inn Premium Gotanda (~$70-85, 2 stops from Shinagawa, one last onsen+ramen)
